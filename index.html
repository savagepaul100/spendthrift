Welcome to SpendThrift

SpendThrift is a clean and powerful expense tracker designed to help you monitor your daily spending with clarity.

Add expenses, categorize them, attach dates, include images, and manage everything in one minimal interface.

Designed for simplicity, built for control.

How It Works

• Title – What you spent money on

• Amount – Money spent on it

• Date & Time – Use current time or custom

• Category – Organize expenses by type

• Note – Optional details

• 3-Dot Menu – Edit or delete any expense
